#### Customize your vehicle jump height, gravity and force

**Active :** Custom values will override the games default vehicle jump settings


**Inactive :** Vehicle jump stats will remain unchanged


Any changes to the vehicle jump settings or toggling the mod **will require** you to respawn your vehicle!
